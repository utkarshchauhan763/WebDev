Local Cricket Team Wins State Championship

October 13, 2025

In a thrilling finale, the Mumbai Warriors defeated the Pune Panthers by 5 wickets to claim their third consecutive state championship title. The match, held at the Wankhede Stadium, saw an outstanding performance from captain Rohit Sharma who scored an unbeaten 89 runs. The victory marks a historic achievement for the team and brings great pride to the city.

New Web Accessibility Standards Announced

October 12, 2025

The W3C has released updated guidelines for web accessibility, emphasizing the importance of semantic HTML and inclusive design practices. The new standards aim to make the internet more accessible to users with disabilities and provide clearer guidance for developers. Major tech companies have already committed to implementing these standards by the end of 2025.

Source: Tech Today

Government Launches Digital Education Initiative

October 11, 2025

The Ministry of Education has unveiled a comprehensive digital education program aimed at providing quality online learning resources to students across the country. The initiative includes free access to coding courses, digital literacy programs, and virtual laboratories. The program is expected to benefit over 10 million students nationwide and bridge the digital divide in education.
